Welcome to Flashcard Master!

Here’s how to use the app to master any topic with smart flashcards:

1. Creating Decks

- Go to the Decks Page and click the Create Deck button.
- Enter a name for your deck and click Create Deck.
- Your new deck will appear in the list on the Decks Page.

2. Adding Flashcards

- Go to the Decks Page and click the Add button in the navigation bar.
- Select a deck from the dropdown menu.
- Enter the Front (question) and Back (answer) of the flashcard.
- Optionally, add Extra Information for additional context.
- Click Add Flashcard to save it to the selected deck.

3. Reviewing Flashcards

- Go to the Decks Page and click on a deck to open the Review Preview Page.
- Click the Study button to start reviewing flashcards.
- On the Review Flashcards Page:
  - The Front of the flashcard is displayed first.
  - Click Show Answer or press the Spacebar to reveal the Back of the flashcard.
  - Optionally, click Show Extra Info to view additional information.
  - Rate your recall using the buttons:
    - Again: If you didn’t remember the answer.
    - Hard: If you remembered the answer with difficulty.
    - Good: If you remembered the answer correctly.
    - Easy: If you remembered the answer easily.
- The app uses spaced repetition to schedule reviews for optimal retention.

4. Managing Decks

- On the Decks Page, you can:
  - Edit a deck by clicking the edit icon.
  - Delete a deck by clicking the delete icon.
  - Organize decks into a hierarchy by dragging and dropping.

5. Logging Out

- Click the Logout button on the Decks Page to log out of your account.
